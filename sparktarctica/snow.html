Captain's Log

Day 45


We've been travelling around the pole for weeks now and I am so sick of snow. They say that every snowflake is unique, but they all look the same to me. I picked up a handful of snow to look at more closely, but I doubt any of them are unique. It's getting hard to think of anything original to write in this log when there's just snow everywhere - I wonder what3words I could come up with?
