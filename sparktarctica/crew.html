Captain's Log

Day 196


It's a big responsibility being the captain of a ship with such a large crew. I've got to make sure I know where everyone is: quartermasters, lieutenants, navigators, engineers, deck cadets, carpenters, first mates, admirals, oilers, cabin boys, watchmen, gunners, bosuns... It's nearly all-consuming, it's a wonder there's space leftover for anything else!
