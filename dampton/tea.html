Escape from Dampton Court

Looking around, this is clearly the location for the famous Dampton Court all-you-can-eat afternoon tea. These tables must have been carved by a master craftsman, but their recent maintenance has been… innovative. Held together by Blu Tack and Polyfilla, it looks like they might collapse if too much weight is placed on any of them. The cake stands have spaces for baked goods, but they’re empty.
