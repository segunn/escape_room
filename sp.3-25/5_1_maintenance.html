Maintenance Bay

Your usually helpful droid assistant is malfunctioning, and seems almost grumpy about something. Despite its lack of legs, it's managing an irritated.stamp.about. You start to feel some remorse for making it do all the boring jobs.

Visit the maintenance bay, place your robot on the dock, and see if you can get to the long and short of what it's so upset about.

Note: Once the message starts, take the robot off the maintenance platform.

If you can answer that question, you'll find the key.
