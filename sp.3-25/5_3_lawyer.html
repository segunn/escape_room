Territorialien

Earth has been asked to act as mediators to help resolve a local territory dispute - and we're sending you!
If you can find each alien a space they're happy to stand in, we'll be able to disperse the gathering.alien.lawyer.

The rules are...

  • No two aliens can stand on the same square
  • No two aliens can be able to see each other
  • Each alien watches all the squares they can see
  • Every square without a tower must be watched by at least one alien
  • Aliens can see in straight lines to their north, south, east and west.
  • Aliens cannot see diagonally
  • Aliens cannot see through towers
  • If a tower has a number on it, that is how many aliens in total must be placed in the squares to the north, south, east and west.
