Multiverse

They're not quite what we're used to - there's one or two letters changed in their name, making them slightly different. We don't know what triggered it - could be cosmic rays, could be a supermassive black hole, could be asteroid.enveloped.ordeals?

If you can track down the objects and work out the new letters in their names, perhaps you'll see the cause?
