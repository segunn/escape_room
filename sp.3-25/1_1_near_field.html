Near Field Cowmunication

Ancient Earth legends tell of a cow that jumped over the moon. What they don't usually record is how far it went on to travel.
In fact, it landed on a planet near here, which is now populated entirely by cows. Now it's time for you to visit, and find out how our cattle.storyline.speeds.

Find the cows and scan them with your telecommunications device. (You might need an android). The cows will give you clues about what order they should go in.

The numbers when the cows are in order will spell out the (numerical!) key.
